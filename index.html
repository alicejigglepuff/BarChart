Growth of U.S. Electric Fueling Stations

Data Source(data last updated September, 2016): either from U.S. Department of Energy or from historical Transportation Energy Data Books.

This chart shows the trend of U.S. electric fueling stations from 1992 to 2016. The growth in electric vehicle supply equipment (EVSE) accelerated starting in 2012, following the 2010 increase of plug-in electric vehicles offered by major automakers. 2016 experienced the largest growth for EVSE in order to support the growing electric vehicle population. (Source: U.S. Department of Energy)
